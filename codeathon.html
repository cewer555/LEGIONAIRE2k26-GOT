Codeathon Laws

CODEATHON

Laws & Decrees of Winterfell

Team Details

Each team must have exactly 3 members.
Team members can belong to the different institution.
Only one team per participant.
Team composition cannot be changed after registration.

Event Format

Round 1
⏳ 2 Hours

4 Problems: 1 Easy,1 intermediate, 1 Medium, 1 Hard

Coding Rules

Only organizer-approved languages are allowed.
All code must be written during the event.
Pre-written code and external libraries are prohibited.
All solutions must be original.

No AI Policy

AI tools/chatbots are not allowed.
Mobile phones, smart devices, and external storage are banned.
Only the offline IDE/compiler provided by organizers may be used.
⚠ Violation results in immediate disqualification.

Team Collaboration

Discussion within the team is allowed.
Inter-team communication is prohibited.
One system per team unless stated otherwise.

Scoring & Evaluation

Points based on problem difficulty.
Rankings based on score and submission time.
Tie-breaker: Higher Round 2 score, then earlier submission.

Disqualification

Use of AI, plagiarism, or unfair practices.
Violators will be banished from the realm immediately.

Code of Conduct

Maintain discipline and professionalism.
Judges' and organizers' decisions are final.

Results & Certificates

Winners decided based on Round 2 performance.
Certificates for finalists and winners.

Prize Money

Winner – ₹2,000
Runner-up – ₹1,000
