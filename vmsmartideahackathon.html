Vm Ideathon

Laws & Decrees of the Kings Landing

Team Details

Each team can have upto 3 members.
Team members can belong todifferent institution.
Only one team per participant.
Team composition cannot be changed after registration.

Event Format

Ideathon is an idea-presentation–only event.
Team size must be 2 or 3 members.
A maximum of 30 teams can participate.
Each team must submit a real-world problem statement and proposed solution during registration.
Incomplete, unclear, or irrelevant submissions will be rejected.

Event Rounds

Round 1 — Elimination Round

Evaluation Criteria: Problem relevance, innovation, feasibility, and solution clarity.

Shortlisting: Only the Top 10 teams will qualify for the final round.

Final Round — Idea Presentation

Shortlisted teams must present their ideas in detail.

Evaluation by field-experience judges.

Winner and Runner-up selected based on overall performance.

Ideathon Rules

Only registered teams are allowed to participate.
Team members cannot be changed after registration.
Problem statements must address a real-world issue.
Ideas must be original; plagiarism leads to immediate disqualification.
Participants must follow the allocated presentation time strictly.
Any form of unfair means or misconduct results in elimination.
Judges’ decisions are final and binding.

Prize Money

Winner – ₹2,000
Runner-up – ₹1,000
